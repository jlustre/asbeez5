# AsBeez Group – Investor FAQ & Due Diligence Pack
## Comprehensive Q&A for Prospective Investors

---

## 1. Overview & Vision

### Q1: What is AsBeez Group?
**AsBeez Group** is an integrated business ecosystem combining:
- grocery retail
- bakery
- fastfood
- rice retail
- rental apartments
- poultry farming

All businesses are unified by a proprietary loyalty engine—the **AsBeez Loyalty Reward System (ALRS)**—designed to drive repeat purchases and sustainable growth.

---

### Q2: What problem does AsBeez solve?
- Fragmented retail experiences
- Low customer loyalty in price-driven markets
- Rising marketing and customer acquisition costs
- Volatile single-income business models

AsBeez solves these by combining **essential daily-need businesses** with a **profit-funded loyalty system**.

## 5. AsBeez Loyalty Reward System (ALRS)

### Q9: What is ALRS?
ALRS is a **customer loyalty rewards system** that:
- rewards shopping behavior
- increases repeat purchases
- funds rewards from real profit margins
- converts rewards to store credit only

---

### Q10: Is ALRS an MLM or investment scheme?
No. ALRS:
- has no recruiting
- has no joining fees
- pays no cash
- offers no equity or income promises
- rewards only retail transactions

It is a loyalty program, not a business opportunity.

---

### Q11: How does ALRS help investors?
ALRS:
- lowers customer acquisition cost
- increases customer lifetime value
- stabilizes revenue
- improves unit economics
- creates brand stickiness
