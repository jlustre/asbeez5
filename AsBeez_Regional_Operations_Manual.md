# ASBEEZ REGIONAL OPERATIONS MANUAL

### Purpose
Ensure consistent execution, compliance, and profitability across regions.

### Regional Roles
- Regional Operations Manager
- Trainer / QA
- Finance Liaison

### Responsibilities
- SOP enforcement
- Financial oversight
- Training & audits

### KPIs
- Sales growth
- Shrinkage %
- Cash variance
- SOP compliance

### Governance Rule
Regions execute. HQ decides. Systems enforce.

### Acknowledgment
All Regional Managers must comply with this manual.
