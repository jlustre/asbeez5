# AsBeez Business Cell (ARC) Compensation Plan  
**(Points-Based Version – Currency Conversion Only Upon Claim and Approval)**

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope
This Compensation Plan defines how AsBeez rewards participating customers (“Members”) for qualifying purchase activity on the AsBeez platform. The plan operates as a **purchase-based, points-driven loyalty and reward system**.

All rewards are **earned, tracked, and accumulated exclusively in AsBeez Business Cell Points (ARCP)**. Conversion of ARCP into currency occurs **only when a reward claim is submitted and approved**, in accordance with this plan.

AsBeez operates as a marketplace facilitator and is not responsible for vendor product fulfillment or delivery unless expressly stated otherwise.

---

## 2. Definitions

- **AsBeez** – The company operating the AsBeez e-commerce platform.  
- **Member** – A registered customer with an active AsBeez account.  
- **Vendor** – A third-party seller offering products or services through the AsBeez platform.  
- **AsBeez Business Cell Points (ARCP)** – Internal, non-cash points generated from qualifying purchases and used for ARC generation, reward distribution, and accounting purposes.  
- **AsBeez Business Cell (ARC)** – A digital reward position earned by a Member upon accumulating qualifying ARCP, used solely for reward calculation and tracking.  
- **Placement Structure** – A three-wide (3×∞), forced-fill placement structure used to determine ARC placement and reward eligibility.  
- **Upline ARCs** – ARCs positioned above a newly created ARC in the placement structure, determined by direct parent relationships.  
- **Qualified Level** – An upline level that exists and is eligible to receive Reward Points at the time a new ARC is created.

---

## 3. Earning AsBeez Business Cell Points (ARCP)
1. Each product or service offered on the AsBeez platform may be assigned an ARCP value.  
2. ARCP are generated only from **completed and paid retail purchases**.  
3. ARCP:
   - Are non-cash and non-transferable  
   - Have no intrinsic monetary value prior to claim  
   - Exist solely for internal reward tracking and eligibility determination  

---

## 4. Generation of AsBeez Business Cells (ARCs)
1. For every **1,000 ARCP** accumulated by a Member, the Member earns **one (1) AsBeez Business Cell (ARC)**.  
2. ARCP in excess of a 1,000-point multiple are carried forward until sufficient to generate additional ARCs.  
3. Members may earn **multiple ARCs** over time based exclusively on qualifying purchase activity.

---

## 5. Placement of ARCs
1. All ARCs are placed into a **single, global placement structure** maintained by AsBeez.  
2. The placement structure:
   - Is three-wide (each ARC may have up to three direct child ARCs)  
   - Has unlimited depth  
3. Placement is automated using a **top-to-bottom, left-to-right (breadth-first)** method based on the order of ARC creation.  
4. Placement cannot be influenced, reserved, transferred, or altered by Members.

---

## 6. ARCP Distribution Upon ARC Creation

### 6.1 Distribution Pool
1. Each time a new ARC is created as a result of **1,000 ARCP**, a total of **600 ARCP** is allocated for reward distribution.  
2. The remaining **400 ARCP** are retained by **AsBeez as company revenue**.

---

### 6.2 Upline Distribution Rules
1. The 600-point distribution pool is shared among the **owners of up to twelve (12) qualified upline ARCs**.  
2. Each qualified upline level receives **50 ARCP per level**.  
3. Distribution is limited to a maximum of **twelve (12) upline levels**, for a total potential distribution of **600 ARCP**.

---

### 6.3 Insufficient Qualified Levels
1. If fewer than twelve (12) qualified upline ARCs exist at the time a new ARC is created:
   - ARCP are distributed only to the existing qualified uplines  
   - Any undistributed portion of the 600-point pool is retained by **AsBeez**  
2. Undistributed ARCP are not deferred, accumulated, or reassigned.

---

## 7. Reward Accumulation, Claim Threshold, and Conversion

### 7.1 Accumulation
1. All rewards are accumulated and tracked as **AsBeez Business Cell Points (ARCP)**.  
2. ARCP continue to accrue from ARC activity until a claim is submitted.

### 7.2 Claim Threshold
1. A Member must accumulate a minimum of **5,000 ARCP** to become eligible to submit a reward claim.  
2. ARCP below the threshold remain accrued and are not forfeited.

### 7.3 Conversion Ratio (Upon Claim and Approval)
1. Upon claim submission and approval, ARCP are converted to currency at the following fixed ratio:

   **100 ARCP = $1.00 USD**

2. Conversion occurs **only after approval** and is subject to verification, compliance checks, and payout processing.  
3. Prior to conversion, ARCP:
   - Are not currency  
   - Are not redeemable  
   - Do not represent stored value or cash equivalents  

---

## 8. Maximum Potential Per ARC (Points-Based)
1. Although the placement structure is infinite, rewards for any individual ARC are limited to activity occurring within **twelve (12) levels below that ARC**.  
2. In a three-wide structure, the total number of potential descendant ARCs within twelve levels is **797,160**.  
3. At **50 ARCP per qualifying descendant event**, the maximum cumulative reward potential for a single ARC is:

   **39,858,000 ARCP**

4. Using the approved conversion ratio, this represents a **theoretical maximum of $398,580.00 USD**, which does not guarantee conversion or payout.

---

## 9. Ownership, Reversion, and Earnings Attribution
1. ARCP are credited to the **owner of each eligible ARC**, not based on recruitment or sponsorship relationships.  
2. A Member may own multiple ARCs, each accruing ARCP independently.  
3. If an ARC is created as a result of a transaction that is later **cancelled, refunded, returned, charged back, or invalidated**:
   - The associated ARC may be **reverted**  
   - Ownership of the reverted ARC transfers permanently to **AsBeez**  
   - Any ARCP generated by reverted ARCs accrue to AsBeez as company-owned points  
4. Members retain no rights to reverted ARCs or future rewards derived from them.

---

## 10. Recordkeeping and Auditability
AsBeez maintains system-generated records of:
- ARCP earned per transaction  
- ARCs generated and owned  
- Placement and upline relationships  
- ARCP accrued per ARC and per Member  
- Claimed, approved, and converted rewards  
- Company-retained ARCP  

All records are maintained for transparency, auditability, and regulatory compliance.

---

## 11. Modifications and Termination
AsBeez reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate this Compensation Plan at any time, subject to applicable laws and notice requirements. Continued participation constitutes acceptance of such modifications.

---

## 12. Disclaimer
Participation in the AsBeez Compensation Plan does not guarantee income. ARCP are an internal accounting mechanism only and do not constitute cash, currency, stored value, or financial instruments until converted upon approved claim.

# Appendix A — Compliance and Regulatory Considerations

## A.1 Purchase-Based Rewards Only
1. Participation does not require enrollment or participation fees.  
2. ARCs and ARCP are generated **solely from qualifying retail purchases**.  
3. No rewards are paid for recruiting, sponsoring, or enrolling other participants.

---

## A.2 No Investment or Income Guarantee
1. This plan is **not an investment program**.  
2. ARCP and ARCs do not represent securities, equity, or ownership interests.  
3. All earning examples are illustrative and non-guaranteed.

---

## A.3 Reverted Transactions and Company Ownership
1. Cancelled, refunded, returned, or charged-back transactions may result in:
   - ARCP reversal  
   - ARC reversion  
2. Reverted ARCs become company-owned and may continue to accrue ARCP for AsBeez.

---

## A.4 Disbursement and Conversion Controls
1. Conversion from ARCP to currency occurs **only after claim approval**.  
2. Conversion is subject to identity verification, fraud review, compliance checks, and payout processing requirements.

---

## A.5 Jurisdictional Compliance
1. Loyalty and reward programs are subject to varying jurisdictional regulations.  
2. AsBeez reserves the right to modify participation or conversion rules to comply with applicable laws.
