# AsBeez Business Cell (ABC) Compensation Plan  
**(Points-Based Version – Currency Conversion Only Upon Claim and Approval)**

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope
This Compensation Plan defines how AsBeez rewards participating customers (“Members”) for qualifying purchase activity on the AsBeez platform. The plan operates as a **purchase-based, points-driven loyalty and reward system**.

All rewards are **earned, tracked, and accumulated exclusively in AsBeez Business Cell Points (ABCP)**. Conversion of ABCP into currency occurs **only when a reward claim is submitted and approved**, in accordance with this plan.

AsBeez operates as a marketplace facilitator and is not responsible for vendor product fulfillment or delivery unless expressly stated otherwise.

---

## 2. Definitions

- **AsBeez** – The company operating the AsBeez e-commerce platform.  
- **Member** – A registered customer with an active AsBeez account.  
- **Vendor** – A third-party seller offering products or services through the AsBeez platform.  
- **AsBeez Business Cell Points (ABCP)** – Internal, non-cash points generated from qualifying purchases and used for ABC generation, reward distribution, and accounting purposes.  
- **AsBeez Business Cell (ABC)** – A digital reward position earned by a Member upon accumulating qualifying ABCP, used solely for reward calculation and tracking.  
- **Placement Structure** – A three-wide (3×∞), forced-fill placement structure used to determine ABC placement and reward eligibility.  
- **Upline ABCs** – ABCs positioned above a newly created ABC in the placement structure, determined by direct parent relationships.  
- **Qualified Level** – An upline level that exists and is eligible to receive Business Points at the time a new ABC is created.

---

## 3. Earning AsBeez Business Cell Points (ABCP)
1. Each product or service offered on the AsBeez platform may be assigned an ABCP value.  
2. ABCP are generated only from **completed and paid retail purchases**.  
3. ABCP:
   - Are non-cash and non-transferable  
   - Have no intrinsic monetary value prior to claim  
   - Exist solely for internal reward tracking and eligibility determination  

---

## 4. Generation of AsBeez Business Cells (ABCs)
1. For every **1,000 ABCP** accumulated by a Member, the Member earns **one (1) AsBeez Business Cell (ABC)**.  
2. ABCP in excess of a 1,000-point multiple are carried forward until sufficient to generate additional ABCs.  
3. Members may earn **multiple ABCs** over time based exclusively on qualifying purchase activity.

---

## 5. Placement of ABCs
1. All ABCs are placed into a **single, global placement structure** maintained by AsBeez.  
2. The placement structure:
   - Is three-wide (each ABC may have up to three direct child ABCs)  
   - Has unlimited depth  
3. Placement is automated using a **top-to-bottom, left-to-right (breadth-first)** method based on the order of ABC creation.  
4. Placement cannot be influenced, reserved, transferred, or altered by Members.

---

## 6. ABCP Distribution Upon ABC Creation

### 6.1 Distribution Pool
1. Each time a new ABC is created as a result of **1,000 ABCP**, a total of **600 ABCP** is allocated for reward distribution.  
2. The remaining **400 ABCP** are retained by **AsBeez as company revenue**.

---

### 6.2 Upline Distribution Rules
1. The 600-point distribution pool is shared among the **owners of up to twelve (12) qualified upline ABCs**.  
2. Each qualified upline level receives **50 ABCP per level**.  
3. Distribution is limited to a maximum of **twelve (12) upline levels**, for a total potential distribution of **600 ABCP**.

---

### 6.3 Insufficient Qualified Levels
1. If fewer than twelve (12) qualified upline ABCs exist at the time a new ABC is created:
   - ABCP are distributed only to the existing qualified uplines  
   - Any undistributed portion of the 600-point pool is retained by **AsBeez**  
2. Undistributed ABCP are not deferred, accumulated, or reassigned.

---

## 7. Reward Accumulation, Claim Threshold, and Conversion

### 7.1 Accumulation
1. All rewards are accumulated and tracked as **AsBeez Business Cell Points (ABCP)**.  
2. ABCP continue to accrue from ABC activity until a claim is submitted.

### 7.2 Claim Threshold
1. A Member must accumulate a minimum of **5,000 ABCP** to become eligible to submit a reward claim.  
2. ABCP below the threshold remain accrued and are not forfeited.

### 7.3 Conversion Ratio (Upon Claim and Approval)
1. Upon claim submission and approval, ABCP are converted to currency at the following fixed ratio:

   **100 ABCP = $1.00 USD**

2. Conversion occurs **only after approval** and is subject to verification, compliance checks, and payout processing.  
3. Prior to conversion, ABCP:
   - Are not currency  
   - Are not redeemable  
   - Do not represent stored value or cash equivalents  

---

## 8. Maximum Potential Per ABC (Points-Based)
1. Although the placement structure is infinite, rewards for any individual ABC are limited to activity occurring within **twelve (12) levels below that ABC**.  
2. In a three-wide structure, the total number of potential descendant ABCs within twelve levels is **797,160**.  
3. At **50 ABCP per qualifying descendant event**, the maximum cumulative reward potential for a single ABC is:

   **39,858,000 ABCP**

4. Using the approved conversion ratio, this represents a **theoretical maximum of $398,580.00 USD**, which does not guarantee conversion or payout.

---

## 9. Ownership, Reversion, and Earnings Attribution
1. ABCP are credited to the **owner of each eligible ABC**, not based on recruitment or sponsorship relationships.  
2. A Member may own multiple ABCs, each accruing ABCP independently.  
3. If an ABC is created as a result of a transaction that is later **cancelled, refunded, returned, charged back, or invalidated**:
   - The associated ABC may be **reverted**  
   - Ownership of the reverted ABC transfers permanently to **AsBeez**  
   - Any ABCP generated by reverted ABCs accrue to AsBeez as company-owned points  
4. Members retain no rights to reverted ABCs or future rewards derived from them.

---

## 10. Recordkeeping and Auditability
AsBeez maintains system-generated records of:
- ABCP earned per transaction  
- ABCs generated and owned  
- Placement and upline relationships  
- ABCP accrued per ABC and per Member  
- Claimed, approved, and converted rewards  
- Company-retained ABCP  

All records are maintained for transparency, auditability, and regulatory compliance.

---

## 11. Modifications and Termination
AsBeez reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate this Compensation Plan at any time, subject to applicable laws and notice requirements. Continued participation constitutes acceptance of such modifications.

---

## 12. Disclaimer
Participation in the AsBeez Compensation Plan does not guarantee income. ABCP are an internal accounting mechanism only and do not constitute cash, currency, stored value, or financial instruments until converted upon approved claim.

3. No rewards are paid for recruiting, sponsoring, or enrolling other participants.

---

## A.2 No Investment or Income Guarantee
1. This plan is **not an investment program**.  
2. ABCP and ABCs do not represent securities, equity, or ownership interests.
