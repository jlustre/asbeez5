# ASBEEZ FOUNDER HANDS-OFF OPERATING DASHBOARD SPEC

## Purpose
Provide founder with full visibility while avoiding operational involvement.

## Design Principles
- Exception-first
- Read-only
- Trend-based

## Dashboard Sections
1. System Health (Green/Yellow/Red)
2. Financial Snapshot
3. Exceptions & Risks
4. Franchise Health Index
5. Regional Performance
6. Growth Metrics

## Alerting Rules
Alerts trigger only on repeated or severe exceptions.

## Founder Workflow
- Daily: 5-minute scan
- Weekly: trend review
- Monthly: board-style review

## Success Definition
“I know exactly what’s happening without touching operations.”
